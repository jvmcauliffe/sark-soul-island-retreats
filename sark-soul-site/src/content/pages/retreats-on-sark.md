This page carries the booking copy migrated from the live site. It is a holding version until the live copy is pasted in; the dates, prices and booking structure are final.

The Late Summer Retreat runs from 12 to 17 September 2026 on the Isle of Sark, Channel Islands. Getting here is part of the retreat: a [ferry from Guernsey](https://sarkshipping.gg) takes around 55 minutes, the tractor-drawn toast rack brings you up from the harbour, and a horse and carriage carries you on to the retreat house.

Choose the retreat that speaks to you: [yoga](/yoga-retreat-sark), [digital detox](/digital-detox-retreat), [coming on your own](/solo-retreat-women), [burnout recovery](/burnout-retreat) or [dark skies](/dark-sky-retreat). Every one is the same five nights, the same house and the same table.

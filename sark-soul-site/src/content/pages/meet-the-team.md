**Nadia, founder and host.** Nadia founded Sark Soul Island Retreats and hosts every retreat herself, from the first welcome at the harbour to the last carriage back down.

**Monica, senior yoga teacher.** Monica of YogaMorphic leads the yoga on our retreats, a senior teacher with some thirty years of experience. Read more about her approach on [The Practice](/the-practice).

**Bram, chef.** Bram cooks the vegetarian meals the retreats are known for, generous and seasonal, served family style around one long table.

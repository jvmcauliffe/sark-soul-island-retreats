Fly into Guernsey, around 45 minutes from London, or sail from the UK south coast. From Guernsey, the [passenger ferry](https://sarkshipping.gg) crosses to Sark in around 55 minutes, passing Herm and Jethou on the way.

Arrival on Sark comes in two parts guests never forget: a tractor-drawn toast rack carries you up Harbour Hill, then a horse and carriage brings you to the retreat house.

We help every guest plan the journey when they book. Start on [the booking page](/retreats-on-sark), or download the full guide below.

## Arriving

> "Nadia met us as we got off the ferry. She could not have been more
> welcoming."
>
> Yvonne, May 2026

> "I have thoroughly enjoyed this retreat. There is plenty to do or you
> can just relax. Very peaceful without traffic."
>
> Maxine, May 2026

Nadia's line: The crossing does half the work before we even say hello.
By the time the carriage turns up the lane, the mainland has let go.

## The mornings

> "Monica is a truly gifted teacher. Yoga was well paced and thoughtfully
> put together to accommodate all levels. Each day we explored something
> new, so gave us a great variety in the practice and also knowledge to
> take away for our own practice."
>
> Annie, May 2026

> "I was a bit concerned I wouldn't be able to do lots of yoga as I was
> concerned about my knee and hip, but the pace has been perfect for me.
> There has been one challenge which I have enjoyed, headstands! But the
> restorative evening practice has been wonderful."
>
> Amanda, May 2026

Nadia's line: We begin gently. The studio door open, the garden just
waking, and no reason to hurry anything.

## The days

> "I didn't know I liked walking so much. Nadia and John were a wealth of
> knowledge which made the walks really interesting."
>
> Annie, May 2026

> "I enjoyed the walks as you know the areas and John's talks. Although
> a small island, there is plenty to see. The massage was an aid to
> feeling relaxed."
>
> Maxine, May 2026

Nadia's line: The afternoons belong to you. Some guests walk every
headland; some barely leave the garden. Both are doing it right, and
every activity is entirely optional.

## The evenings

> "The food and the chefs were fabulous. The variety across the week was
> incredible and they were a joy to be around."
>
> Andrea, May 2026

> "Food was interesting. Lots of dishes were a first for me! I loved the
> soups."
>
> Yvonne, May 2026

Nadia's line: When the lights never come on, the sky does. Dinner runs
long here, and nobody reaches for a phone.

## Leaving

> "I can't think of anything as every day was a new adventure, as
> enjoyable as the previous one. This week has been overwhelming and
> precious."
>
> Maria, May 2026

> "Sark is a very special place. I will definitely be coming back. My
> only regret is I didn't have more time to appreciate it and savour it."

> Amanda, May 2026

## The mornings

> "Monica is a truly gifted teacher. Yoga was well paced and thoughtfully

> take away for our own practice."
>
> Annie, May 2026

> "I was a bit concerned I wouldn't be able to do lots of yoga as I was

>

waking, and no reason to hurry anything.

## The days

> "I didn't know I liked walking so much. Nadia and John were a wealth of
> knowledge which made the walks really interesting."
>
> Annie, May 2026

> "Sark is a very special place. I will definitely be coming back. My
> only regret is I didn't have more time to appreciate it and savour it."
>
> Amanda, May 2026

Nadia's line: The afternoons belong to you. Some guests walk every
headland; some barely leave the garden. Both are doing it right, and
every activity is entirely optional.

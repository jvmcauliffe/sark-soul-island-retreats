THE SETTING

# The world's first Dark Sky Island

Sark Soul Island Retreats hosts intimate yoga retreats on Sark Island in the Channel Islands. Our small group immersions are designed for those seeking deep rest, reconnection and space away from mainland pace. This yoga retreat welcomes both experienced practitioners and curious newcomers.

From the moment you step off the boat, distraction falls away. There are no cars, no streetlights, no rush. Daily yoga, coastal walks and nourishing seasonal food support genuine restoration, and when night falls, the world’s first Dark Sky Island reveals its greatest gift.

THE PRACTICE

# Led by Monica, our teacher

Each day opens and closes with yoga. Morning practice to wake the body gently, evening practice to let the day settle. Monica Marini leads every session, drawing on around thirty years of teaching. Her style is warm and unhurried, with room for both seasoned practitioners and those returning to the mat after time away.



You will never be pushed to perform. Sessions are small by design, so the teaching meets you where you are. Between practices, the days are yours: coastal walks, sea swims, quiet corners for rest.

THE FOUNDER

# Created and Hosted by Nadia

Sark Soul Island Retreats was founded by Nadia, who fell for the island's stillness and built a retreat around it. She hosts every gathering personally - and she's who you'll be speaking to from your first enquiry to your last morning.

- A place to arrive -

Held within a historic farmhouse surrounded by stunning gardens, this is more than a beautiful setting, it is a place that naturally slows the body and quiets the mind. The setting supports rest, reflection, and a gentle return to yourself.

## Come join us

###### - A yoga retreat in the Channel Islands -

Sark sits a short ferry from Guernsey, half a day from London and a world away from everything else. Plenty of guests first meet the island on a day trip, then find themselves wanting more than a few hours. A week is how you really feel the place settle into you.

- Balancing practice & ease -

Spacious days, time to rest, sea air, coastal walks, turquoise coves, daily swims, shared meals, with morning and evening Yoga framing the day. Life on Sark is simple and unhurried, offering space to breathe, move, and settle.

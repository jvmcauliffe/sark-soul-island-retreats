## Getting to Sark

Reaching Sark is part of the retreat experience - a jewel reached only by boat. From Guernsey, the crossing takes around 55 minutes, gently easing you from everyday pace into island life.



Step 1: Travel to Guernsey Guernsey is the main gateway to Sark. There are direct flights from the UK and Europe, including Aurigny, British Airways and Loganair. Allow around 20 - 30 minutes to travel from Guernsey Airport to the ferry by taxi or bus. You can also travel by ferry from the UK or France if you prefer a sea crossing.



Step 2: Ferry from Guernsey to Sark From Guernsey, a passenger ferry takes you to Sark in around 55 minutes, with islands unfolding on the horizon. Ferry times vary by day, and we will share recommended sailings once your booking is confirmed.



Step 3: Arrival on Sark

Sark is car-free. On arrival, your luggage is taken care of, and you travel up from the harbour by the island's tractor-drawn toast rack, then on to the farmhouse by horse and carriage.



Please note that the last ferry to Sark departs at 4pm. Full travel guidance will be shared with all confirmed guests to make planning as smooth as possible.

The Late Summer Retreat runs from 12 to 17 September 2026 on the Isle of Sark, Channel Islands. Getting here is part of the retreat: a ferry from Guernsey with [Sark Shipping](https://sarkshipping.gg) takes around 55 minutes, the tractor-drawn toast rack brings you up from the harbour, and a horse and carriage carries you on to the retreat house.
